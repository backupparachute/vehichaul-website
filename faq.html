Have questions? Browse through our FAQ page to find the answer and understand why VehicHaul is the best choice to simplify mobile delivery visibility. If you can't find the answer here, feel free to contact us to discuss how VehicHaul can meet your delivery tracking management needs.

  • Is there a limit to the number of VINs?

    No. VehicHaul allows users to input data and track an unlimited number of VINs. Whereas other mobile delivery apps charge by VIN, VehicHaul does not.

  • Is there a limit to the number of users who can access our account?

    For enterprise customers, there is no limit to the number of users, drivers, or customers that can access the account.

  • What type of support is available for the VehicHaul app?

    Although VehicHaul is diverse and equipped to complement your existing delivery tracking system, our professionals are happy to implement customized options.

  • How is the data stored and backed up?

    We use the latest in cloud technology to keep your data safe and secure at all times.

  • How do I set up the system?

    VehicHaul staff will work closely with each client to set up the system and integrate the app with your existing vehicle transportation management software.

  • Is customization available for the VehicHaul app?

    The VehicHaul app is fully customizable. Customized options may require additional charges.

  • Does VehicHaul integrate with our existing tracking system?

    Yes, VehicHaul enhances your existing transportation management system by utilizing mobile technology to make your business operations more visible, streamlined and simple.

  • Do you have a demo / training video of the mobile application?

    Yes, you may also Contact us to setup a personalized demo or in the field training.
